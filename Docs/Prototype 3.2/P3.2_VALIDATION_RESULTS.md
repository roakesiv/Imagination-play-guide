## 3. WP1 Validation - Prompt-Only Workflow

### Goal

Make it obvious that the app creates a prompt to copy into ChatGPT or another AI image creator, not an image directly.

### Change Under Test

The generated prompt area now includes:

```text
Press Button to Copy this prompt. Then paste it into ChatGPT or another AI image creator.
The app does the prompt engineering, no images atm.
```

The hero intro also says the app makes a prompt to copy into ChatGPT or a favorite AI image creator.

### Result

**Pass.**

### Evidence / Notes

- WP1 was reviewed and accepted by the product owner.
- Test result provided: complete and passed.
- Prompt-only workflow is now explicit near the generated Image Prompt area.

### Regressions Found

None reported.

### Follow-Up

- Re-check readability after WP5, because header/background changes may affect how the intro copy reads.
